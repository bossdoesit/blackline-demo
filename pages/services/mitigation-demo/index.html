What We Handle

The Mitigation Demo Scopes We Cover.

Mitigation demo sits at the intersection of demolition and remediation — and it requires a crew that understands both sides of that line. Blackline Demo handles the demolition component of mitigation projects involving asbestos-containing materials, mold, water damage, and fire damage. We work within established containment protocols, coordinate with licensed abatement contractors where required, and document our work to the standard your project file demands.

01
Asbestos Mitigation Demo

Selective demolition of materials containing asbestos, coordinated with licensed abatement. We handle the teardown — safely, within containment, on your schedule.

02
Mold Remediation Demo

Demolition of mold-affected structural components and finishes. We remove what the remediation scope identifies and hand off a site ready for treatment and rebuild.

03
Water Damage Demo

Rapid-response demolition for water-damaged materials — drywall, flooring, framing, and cabinetry. We move fast without creating new problems for the mitigation team behind us.

04
Fire Damage Demo

Structural and finish demolition on fire-damaged properties. We clear what's unsalvageable, protect what isn't, and stage the site for restoration.

Who We Work With

Built for Restoration Firms and GCs Managing Mitigation Scopes.

Mitigation projects move differently than standard demolition jobs. There are insurance adjusters, restoration timelines, containment requirements, and documentation standards that don't exist on a typical demo scope. Blackline Demo understands that environment. We've worked within it and we know what it costs a GC or restoration firm when the demo crew doesn't.

Restoration Companies

We fit into your remediation workflow — containment-aware, documentation-ready, and coordinated with your abatement timeline. No surprises on site.

General Contractors

When your project has a mitigation phase, we handle the demo side cleanly and hand off a site that's ready for whatever comes next on your schedule.

Property Managers & Owners

For standalone mitigation projects without a GC intermediary, we work directly with property owners and managers. Same standard, same documentation, same accountability.

How We Operate

What Working With Blackline on a Mitigation Scope Looks Like.

1
Scope Review

We review the mitigation report, ask the right questions, and come back with a clear quote. We tell you what we can do, what we'll coordinate, and what falls outside our scope before you commit.

2
Containment Coordination

We work within the containment setup established by the abatement team or set up our own where required. We don't start demo until the site is prepped correctly.

3
Documented Execution

We execute the demolition scope, photograph progress, and maintain documentation throughout. Your project file gets what it needs without you having to chase it.

4
Clean Handoff

Site cleared, debris removed, ready for the remediation or rebuild phase. We don't leave loose ends that become your problem.
