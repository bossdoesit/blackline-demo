Our Story

Why We Built This Company.

Blackline Demo was founded by Derek and Fernando — two professionals who understand demolition from the inside out and who decided the Phoenix market deserved a better option than what was available.

The standard for demolition subcontracting in this market has historically been low. Crews that don't communicate. Scopes that get interpreted loosely. Timelines that slip without notice. We've seen what that costs the contractors who depend on demo as a critical path phase — and we built Blackline to be the company that changes that expectation.

This isn't a side operation. Blackline Demo is a focused demolition business built specifically to serve the contractor and trade professional community in the Phoenix metro.

Our Standards

What We Hold Ourselves To — On Every Project.

We Show Up When We Say We Will

Schedule commitments are treated as contracts — not estimates. Your project timeline is the one we build our schedule around.

We Communicate Before You Have to Ask

Updates, changes, and unexpected site conditions get reported immediately — before they become a problem for you.

We Execute the Scope We Agreed To

No scope creep. No unauthorized decisions. No surprises at the invoice. You know what you're getting before we start.

We Leave the Site Ready for the Next Trade

Clean handoffs are part of what we deliver. Debris cleared, site staged, ready for whoever comes after us.

We're Honest About What We Can Do

If a scope is outside our capabilities, we tell you before you commit to us. Not after we've started.
