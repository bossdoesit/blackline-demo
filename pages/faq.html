Working With Contractors

B2B & Trade Professionals

Do you work directly with general contractors and builders?
+

Yes — and that's our primary focus. Blackline Demo was built to serve GCs, builders, and trade professionals across the Phoenix metro. We work as a demolition subcontractor on GC-led projects and understand how to operate within a construction schedule and site environment.

Can you work on multi-phase or multi-trade projects?
+

Yes. We're experienced working within multi-trade environments where our demo phase connects directly to what comes next. We coordinate with the GC on site access, staging, and handoff timing.

Are you able to provide documentation for our project file?
+

Yes. We can provide licensing, insurance certificates, and scope documentation for your project file. Let us know what you need when you request a quote.

Licensing & Insurance

Licensed. Insured. Documented.

Is Blackline Demo licensed and insured in Arizona?
+

Yes. Blackline Demo is fully licensed and insured in the state of Arizona. Documentation is available on request before we mobilize on any project.

Services & Scope

What We Handle — and What We Don't.

What types of demolition do you handle?
+

Blackline Demo handles mitigation demo (asbestos, mold, water damage, fire damage), interior demolition (selective and full gut), exterior demolition (site clearing, structural, concrete removal), specialty demo, and excavation-related scope.

Do you handle asbestos abatement?
+

We handle the demolition side of mitigation projects that involve asbestos-containing materials, coordinated with a licensed abatement contractor. We do not perform the abatement itself.

Process & Timeline

How We Operate.

How quickly do you respond to quote requests?

Do you handle debris removal and site cleanup?

Yes. Debris removal and site staging are included in our standard scope. We leave the site in the condition the next phase requires.
