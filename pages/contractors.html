Built for General Contractors Who Can't Afford to Find Out Their Demo Crew Isn't Ready.

Blackline Demo was built around one question: what does a general contractor actually need from a demo subcontractor? The answer isn't the lowest bid. It's a crew that shows up, executes the scope, communicates like a professional, and never makes the GC look bad in front of their client.

The Problem

You've Worked With Demo Crews That Made You Look Bad. We Built This Company So That Doesn't Happen.

Every general contractor has a version of this story. The demo crew that showed up late to the most visible phase of the project. The one that left debris staged in the wrong place and held up the concrete pour. The one that found something unexpected inside the wall and just kept going without telling anyone.

Those crews exist because demolition has historically been treated as the lowest-skill trade on a project. Blackline was started by people who believe that's wrong — and who operate accordingly.

We are not the cheapest option in the Phoenix metro. We are the most reliable one.

How We Operate

What Working With Blackline Looks Like on a GC-Led Project.

01
You Send Us the Scope

We review the project details, ask the right questions, and come back with a clear quote. No vague estimates. No numbers that change after you commit.

02
We Confirm the Schedule

Your timeline drives ours. We confirm mobilization dates and check in before the phase begins — you know we're coming before we show up.

03
We Execute

On site, on time, within scope. We communicate if something changes. We don't make unilateral decisions on your project. You stay in control.

04
We Hand Off Clean

Site ready for the next trade. Debris cleared. No loose ends that become your problem. You don't have to come back and manage what we left behind.

The Specifics

What Contractors Ask About Before They Commit.

Scheduling

We build our schedule around yours. Early mobilization, split phases, specific access windows — those are conversations we have before we commit, not problems we create on site.

Communication

You get a direct contact, not a dispatcher. If something changes on our end, you hear about it before it affects your day.

Licensing & Insurance

Full Arizona licensing and insurance. Documentation available on request before we step foot on your project.

Scope Coverage

Mitigation demo, interior demolition, exterior demolition, specialty scopes. If it involves demolition on your project, we likely cover it.

Service Area

Six cities across the Phoenix metro — Phoenix, Mesa, Scottsdale, Gilbert, Chandler, and Casa Grande.
